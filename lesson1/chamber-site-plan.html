Site Name

Nuwakot Chamber of Commerce

This site will be named after Nuwakot district of Nepal

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to contain the information relate to the Chamber of Commerce including but not limited to the name and information of the buisnesses in the district, rules to join the Chamber, events organized by the Chamber and so on.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find Hardware shops in the distrcts?
  • How do I register my business in the Chamber?
  • What perks do I have for being in the Chamber?
  • What event is the Chamber organizing next weekend?
